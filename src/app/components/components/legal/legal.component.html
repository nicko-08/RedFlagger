Frequently Asked Questions

What is RedFlagger?

RedFlagger is a community-driven platform designed to detect, report, and prevent online scams. We utilize crowdsourcing to verify scam reports and provide real-time updates on fraudulent activities.

How can I report a scam?

You can report a scam by submitting details through our platform. Our system reviews reports and validates them with user feedback to ensure accuracy before publishing them.

Is my information secure when using RedFlagger?

Yes. We prioritize user privacy and do not share personal information with third parties. All reports are anonymized to protect user identities.

Our Mission

At RedFlagger, our mission is to cultivate a safer and more transparent online community by harnessing the power of crowdsourcing to detect, report, and prevent scams. In an era where digital fraud is becoming increasingly sophisticated, we believe that knowledge and collective action are the most effective defenses.

We strive to empower individuals by providing reliable, real-time information on emerging online threats. Through community-driven efforts, we aim to build a network of vigilance where users can share verified reports, raise awareness, and contribute to a safer digital environment.

By fostering collaboration, integrity, and accountability, we are committed to protecting individuals from deceptive practices and ensuring that everyone has access to the tools and knowledge needed to navigate the online world securely.

Our Team

At RedFlagger, we are a team of passionate and driven students from Mapúa University, united by a shared commitment to integrity, innovation, and online safety. With diverse backgrounds in technology and problem-solving, we bring together our expertise to develop solutions that combat digital threats and promote a trustworthy online space.

Our team consists of Ricardo Gayagoy, Derven Gonzales, Matthew Josh Payor, and Nicko Rodavia, each contributing unique skills and perspectives to our mission. We believe that technology should empower and protect users, not exploit them. That’s why we dedicate ourselves to building community-driven solutions that expose scams, raise awareness, and equip individuals with the knowledge they need to stay safe online.

As advocates for transparency and ethical digital practices, we stand firmly against fraudulent activities and deceptive schemes. Through our collaborative efforts, we aim to inspire vigilance, encourage responsible technology use, and create a safer digital environment for everyone. We are more than just a team—we are a movement committed to shaping a future where people can navigate the online world with confidence and security.
